Discover the world of Super Mario™!

Step right into familiar locations and environments from the Mushroom Kingdom. Explore, discover, and play amazing interactive games and Key Challenges throughout the land.

Meet Mario and friends face-to-face.

Get ready to meet Mario, Luigi, Princess Peach and Toad. Make sure to capture the moment. Snap a photo with them and if you’re wearing your Power-Up Band™, you may get an enhanced experience.

Dining and Shopping

At SUPER NINTENDO WORLD™, the food is as fun as the play. Plus, you’ll find toys and apparel so you can take a piece of the adventure home with you.

Toadstool Cafe™

Indulge in tasty dishes at Toadstool Cafe™, where you can enjoy Chef Toad’s favorite recipes offered in the Mushroom Kingdom. Gluten-free and vegetarian options are available.

1-UP Factory™

Find the perfect souvenir at the 1-UP Factory™ store. This super-fun store is where you can find all sorts of items from the Mushroom Kingdom! Browse souvenirs and wearable merch to level up your experience.

There's Even More Nintendo-Themed Dining & Shopping Outside SUPER NINTENDO WORLD™

With great items available right now in Universal Studios Hollywood and Universal CityWalk, you can get in on the fun even before you enter the land.
